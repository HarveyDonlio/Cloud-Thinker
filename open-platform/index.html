How to contribute

We appreciate any contributions you make to our application and value every word of your feedback. However, to get the most out of our application, please read our three point guide.

1.

Create

Help us by creating an archive on a subject your passionate about or that you believe others will find useful (e.g. Recipes). Then specify one primary field, typically a title (e.g. Recipes Name) and four other fields which will guide users as to what data to enter (e.g. Ingredients). Now, users from all around the world can instantly submit their own unique information with the ultimate goal of creating a large library on your specified data.
2.

Browse

Our Open Platform may not replace current, generic search engines such as Google anytime soon. However, we believe that we can provide an alternative which simplifies how users can access information and may in some cases be more efficient. Simply search for an archive and browse all of it's data in just a couple of clicks. If the archive you searched for does not exist yet we encourage you to create it yourself as to help develop our current dtatabases.
3.

Contribute

If there is some information that hasn't already been submitted to an archive you are browsing, we encourage you to add it yourself. Read each field carefully to ensure you are inputting the correct data. Our site relies on users like you contributing and so we would appreciate it if you take a mere five minutes of your time to contribute to some existing archives.
